# How to install Potatocraft
Step 1: Install [Prism Launcher](https://prismlauncher.org/download/windows/) \
Step 2: Run the installer \
Step 3: Login with your microsoft account

IF YOU ALREADY HAVE PRISM INSTALLED, THIS IS CRITICAL!!! \
Update the client at the top of your screen! The game will not launch unless your client is updated!

Step 4: Download the Potatocraft.mrpack file from this repo \
Step 5: Click "Add Instance" in the top left corner of the launcher \
Step 6: On the left side of the window, find Import \
Step 7: Now under Import, click Browse, and select the Potatocraft.mrpack file, then click "Ok" in the New Instance window \
Step 8: Once finished, click on the newly created Instance, and click edit \
Step 9: While not mandatory, you should allocate 8192 MB of RAM if possible. If you have 16 GB Total for your system, you can do 6144 MB. Do not allocate more than 8192 MB. \
Step 10: Click Launch \
Step 11: Now looking at the main menu, click Options and go to Resource packs. \
Step 12: Make sure your enabled resourcepacks match these, and are in the same order:

Note: You can pick to add one of the two leaves, or not. The performance difference is negligible. Additionally, you may want different colored Icons for SVC (Simple Voice Chat) so feel free to swap that out, just make sure it is in the same order as before.

Step 13: Click Done, and let the resource packs load. Then go to Video Settings. \
Step 14: At the top, click Shader Packs. Pick one of the two options available. I recommend Solar, as that is what the modpack was designed with in mind, but BSL works too. \
Step 15: Click Apply, then Done. \
Step 16: Now in the Main Menu again, Click on Multiplayer, and click Add Server. \
Step 17: Name the server, and put **209.25.140.27:1027** in for Server Address. \
Step 18: Click Done, and click Join Server.

Welcome to Potato SMP! You are now in the game.
